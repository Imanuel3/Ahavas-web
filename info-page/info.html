The Beginning

Ahava Meshikhi was born in Moscow Russia on the date of 1963 November 18th, she was raised by her grandfather (Chaim) and grandmother (unknown). Her and her family moved to Israel when she was 10 years old and she stayed there until her military service was up, when she was a full grown woman she moved to New York City to teach Hebrew at a Orthodox Jewish school. There she found the church called Times Square Church and became a Christian, this was a big transformation for her because she was born into the Jewish faith and followed it her whole life up to this point. After this she had a yearning in her heart to go back to Israel and to preach to her own people. when she was in Israel she met her husband at a Bible study whose name was Yaakov, after this point Ahava and Yakov went across Israel to try to preach the word of God and to bring people salvation.

The Birth Of Her Children

When she was in Israel three of her oldest children were born and their names are Yaezer John and Imanuel , after these three were born then they all move to Canada where the rest of her children were born Sarah Ruth Samuel Ami Noakh and Yeshua .
